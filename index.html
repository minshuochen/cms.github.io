About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Princeton ECE, working with Prof. Mengdi Wang. I obtained my Ph.D. from Georgia Tech, under the supervision of Prof. Tuo Zhao and Prof. Wenjing Liao. I also received a Master's degree in Electrical Engineering from UCLA, and Bachelor's degree in Electrical and Information Engineering from Zhejiang University. Here is my CV.

My research focuses on developing principled methodologies and theoretical foundations of deep learning. I am particularly interested in

  • function approximation theory of neural networks and their statistical guarantees in learning applications

  • (deep) reinforcement learning

  • nonconvex optimization
