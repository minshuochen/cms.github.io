About Me

I am a third year Ph.D. student in Machine Learning at Georgia Tech. I am advised by Prof. Tuo Zhao. Before joining Georgia Tech, I received Master's degree in Electrical Engineering from UCLA, and Bachelor's degree in Electrical and Information Engineering from Zhejiang University. Here is my CV.

My research focuses on developing principled methodologies and theoretical foundations of deep learning. I am particularly interested in nonconvex stochastic optimization, generalization and representation theories of deep learning.
